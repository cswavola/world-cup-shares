## Key findings

- Only three players ever finish 1st: **Ben (5/8 scenarios)**, **Queen C
  (2/8)**, **Lauren (1/8)**.
- **Queen C's win condition**: Argentina must beat England in the semi *and*
  win the Final. The third-place result doesn't affect her — she's 1st
  either way.
- **Lauren's only path to 1st**: Argentina beats England in the semi,
  England (not Argentina) wins the third-place match, and Spain wins the
  Final.
- **Ryan cannot overtake Ben in any scenario.** His ceiling is 2nd place,
  reached only when England wins both the semi and the Final — and he does
  overtake Lauren in that one scenario, though never Ben.
- **Jess, Tim, and Cosmo have no path to the podium** — their best finishes
  are all 4th place or worse.
